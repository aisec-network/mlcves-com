ML CVEs tracks vulnerabilities in ML and AI infrastructure — PyTorch, TensorFlow, ONNX, vLLM, llama.cpp, Hugging Face transformers, LangChain, and the rest of the AI/ML supply chain — with analysis of exploitability and patch status.

## What we track

- **Framework CVEs** — vulnerabilities in PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX, and major ML libraries, with severity
- **Inference engine CVEs** — vLLM, llama.cpp, ONNX Runtime, and serving infrastructure vulnerabilities
- **Integration library CVEs** — LangChain, LlamaIndex, and LLM framework vulnerabilities
- **Supply chain threats** — malicious packages, model weights, and dataset distribution threats
- **Patch status and remediation** — affected versions, fixed versions, and mitigation guidance

## Who reads this

Security engineers, ML platform teams, and developers responsible for maintaining AI infrastructure. Every CVE entry includes severity assessment, affected versions, and remediation guidance where available.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed for CVE alerts as they publish. To report an untracked CVE affecting ML infrastructure, contact the editorial desk with a CVE ID or advisory link.
